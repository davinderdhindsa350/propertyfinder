REQUEST REFERENCES

With our range of expertly designed reference questionnaires, individuals can send reference requests to nominated referees to receive valuable feedback to support job applications or school admissions. Organizations can also request for job references en masse from the nominated referees of job applicants on this platform. Our use of questionnaires reduces the degree of subjectivity in references and provides a basis for assessing and comparing candidates. Our questionnaires can be customized to solicit referee feedback that relates to requirements of specific job roles or academic programs.

VERIFY REFEREES

References can be relied upon when the issuing referees are proven to be legitimate. After reference questionnaires are filled by nominated referees and forwarded to us, we utilize a range of online and offline tools to verify the legitimacy of the referees thereby enhancing the credibility of references. When the legitimacy of a referee is established, it gives currency to the reference they have issued once for multiple uses. A Reference requested and verified via this platform may be used for several job or school applications saving you the time and stress of having to request multiple references from the same referee.

ANALYSE REFERENCES

Because we deploy questionnaires in our references, we receive feedback that can be analysed thereby providing a basis to assess and compare candidates on the strength of their references. We deploy an AI-powered technology to generate useful insights from completed reference questionnaires to facilitate swift and informed decision-making on candidates’ suitability for job roles or academic programs. The output of the analysis is presented in user dashboards on the platform.

ARCHIVE REFERENCES

References requested via this platform may be saved for future or multiple uses. We acknowledge the fact that potential referees are not always accessible when we need them to provide references on our behalf. That is why this platform makes it possible to request the references now, have the legitimacy of the referees established and archive the references here for future uses even in the absence of the referees. You can build a portfolio of references of all potential referees willing to vouch for you at you past and present workplaces or school. Then, when new potential employers ask for references, you have a choice to select from your portfolio those that are most relevant to the job requirement at hand. Third parties to whom you provide references from your portfolio on this platform may also verify the legitimacy of the references with us.
