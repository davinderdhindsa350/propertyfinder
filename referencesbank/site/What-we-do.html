What we do

We offer expertly designed reference questionnaires with audio-visual capabilities that introduces standardization in the assessment and use of references. Reference requests can be initiated using our range of diverse questionnaires that are customizable to specific job roles and academic disciplines.

We utilize a range of online and offline tools to verify completed reference questionnaires to establish the legitimacy of nominated referees thereby enhancing the credibility of references

We deploy an AI-powered technology to provide useful insights from completed reference questionnaires to facilitate swift and informed decision-making among competing candidates for job roles or university admission.

Completed and verified reference questionnaires are securely saved on the platform for future uses such as job applications, university admissions, etc. even in the absence or demise of the issuing referees.

How it Works

  • Registration

    Register a free account on the References bank platform as an individual

  • Reference Request

    Using reference questionnaires on the platform, send requests to nominated referees to provide references on your behalf.

  • Referee Verification

    Receive real-time updates on the completion of the questionnaires and the subsequent verification process

  • Generation of Insights

    After verification, insights generated from completed questionnaires are made available in the user dashboard on the platform

  • Utility of References

    Users may generate to display QR codes of references on resumes or grant limited digital access to third parties on demand

  • Post-utility Verification

    Third parties to whom users provide references from their portfolio may also verify the legitimacy of the references with us
