JOB APPLICANTS

Job applicants required to provide references in support of employment can use this platform to request for references from proposed referees. Once the reference questionnaires are completed and the legitimacy of the referees established, useful insights are generated and saved on the platform. Applicants can generate to display QR codes of references on resumes, print out copies or grant digital access to third parties on demand.

How it Works

  • Registration

    Register a free account on the References bank platform as an individual

  • Reference Request

    Using reference questionnaires on the platform, send requests to nominated referees to provide references on your behalf.

  • Referee Verification

    Receive real-time updates on the completion of the questionnaires and the subsequent verification process

  • Generation of Insights

    After verification, insights generated from completed questionnaires are made available in the user dashboard on the platform

  • Utility of References

    Users may generate to display QR codes of references on resumes or grant digital access to third parties on demand

  • Post-utility Verification

    Third parties to whom users provide references from their portfolio may also verify the legitimacy of the references with us

Why use References bank?

Accessibility

Saved references are available on our platform at any time and for any purpose even in the absence of the issuing referees

Convenience & Usability

No need to exit a job before requesting for references. We verify the legitimacy of referees at the time of issuance to enhance reliability of references for future uses.

Choice

Users have the option to choose from a portfolio of verified and saved references tailored to specific future job requirements, etc.

Favourable Pricing

Users pay $10 per reference request and an annual subscription fee of $5 for unlimited access to all saved references and post-utility reference verification servicess

Security of Data

The portfolio of verified references including user data is strictly secured in accordance with the data protection act of Canada.
